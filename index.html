Instructions for "Private Call"

Step 1

One of the two person has to intiate a "Private Call" by choosing a username and password. Make sure to remember the password!. The initiator then has to click on "Call someone". You will be taken to a add participant page. The participant will not appear in the list until he has has successfully the step #2.

Step 2

Click on "Private Call" and choose an appropriate username. However while entering a password, make sure to use the same password as the initiator.
Share your chosen username with initiator and click "Wait for a call". You will be directed to a waiting area where you can wait until the initiator adds you to the call.

Step 3

To add a participant, intiator can fill his/her username. Click on "Add participant" and allow all permissions for audio and video.

Step 4

Both the participants will be presented with series of log messages pertaining to the connection. Click "Start video call".

Step 5

Enjoy!
Make sure not to refresh the page.

Instructions for "Group Call"

Step 1

Initiator can start a "Group Call", by first choosing a username and password. Make sure to remember this password. Now click on "Call someone". You will be taken to an add participant page. Participants will appear only after they have completed the #2.

Step 2

To be added as a participant to a "Group Call", choose an appropriate username however make sure to use the password shared by the initiator of the group call.
Share the chosen username with the host. Now click "Wait for a call". You will be directed to a waiting area where you can wait till host has added you.

Step 3

The host can add a participant by filling the appropriate username. Then click "Add participant" and allow all permissions for audio and video.

Step 4

The host can add more participants by using the "Add participant" button

Step 5

Enjoy!
Make sure not to refresh the page.
